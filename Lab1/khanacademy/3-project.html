Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 1/4 cups
Sugar 3/4 cup
Butter 1 cup
Chocolate Chips 2 cups

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 2: Mix flour, sugar, and butter in a bowl.

Step 3: Add chocolate chips and form dough balls.

Step 4: Bake for 10-12 minutes.

Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Eggs 3 large
Pancetta 150g
Parmesan Cheese 1 cup

Step 1: Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.

Step 2: Fry pancetta until crisp.

Step 3: Beat eggs and mix with Parmesan.

Step 4: Combine hot pasta, pancetta, and egg mixture.
